# Website Architecture and Lead Strategy

## Best section order for conversion + time on page

1. Hero with short lead form
2. Trust strip: location, RERA, size range, possession, starting price
3. Project story: garden-living positioning
4. Quick highlights grid
5. Pricing teaser + gated cost sheet CTA
6. Floor-plan selector by configuration
7. Master plan and garden amenities section
8. Lifestyle amenities grid
9. Location advantage with landmark tabs
10. Specifications
11. FAQs
12. Sticky final CTA: price sheet, brochure, site visit, WhatsApp

## CTA system

- Hero: Get Price Sheet
- Sticky mobile footer: Call / WhatsApp / Book Visit
- After pricing: Unlock Full Cost Sheet
- After floor plans: Get Floor Plans on WhatsApp
- After location: Schedule Site Visit
- FAQ/footer: Talk to Project Expert

## Form fields

Use a low-friction first form:
- Name
- Mobile number
- WhatsApp consent checkbox
- Optional email
- Hidden fields: source page, CTA clicked, configuration interested, UTM source/medium/campaign

## High-intent lead magnets

- Latest price sheet
- Brochure PDF
- Floor plans
- Tower-wise availability
- Site visit slot
- Callback from project expert

## Avoid these mistakes

- Do not publish conflicting tower counts without phase labels.
- Do not say RERA “coming soon”; public pages list RERA IDs.
- Do not hard-code detailed prices unless someone will update weekly.
- Do not overuse generic “luxury apartment” copy; the differentiator is garden living.
