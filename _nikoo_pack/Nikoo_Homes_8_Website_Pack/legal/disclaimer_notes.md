# Legal and Publishing Notes

- Verify exact promoter/entity name and RERA certificate before using the project in paid ads or legal footer.
- Use RERA numbers exactly:
  - Phase 1: PRM/KA/RERA/1251/309/PR/070526/008628
  - Phase 2: PRM/KA/RERA/1251/309/PR/070526/008629
- Do not publish “RERA coming soon”.
- Mention prices as indicative unless a latest approved price sheet is available.
- Mention all areas as indicative; layouts and specifications can change.
- Use a disclaimer near pricing, floor plans and footer.
