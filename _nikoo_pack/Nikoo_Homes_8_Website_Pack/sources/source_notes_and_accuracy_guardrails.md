# Source Notes and Accuracy Guardrails

This pack reconciles data from the user-provided sites plus the public Nikoo Homes 8 page.

## Main source hierarchy

1. Official/public Nikoo Homes 8 page for project story, unit-plan data, RERA numbers, landscape/master-plan vocabulary and brochure URL.
2. Homz N Space for configuration, amenities, specifications, price table, address and location context.
3. Housiey for cross-checking land parcel, towers/floors, unit ranges, RERA possession, target possession, pricing, amenities and RERA links.
4. Partner landing pages only for conversion patterns and additional cross-checking, not as final legal source.

## Important conflicts

- Land parcel appears as both 11.35 acres and 12 acres. Website-safe wording: “about 12 acres”.
- Unit count appears as about 500 homes for Phase 1 and about 1010 homes for the wider development. Label phase-level and overall-level facts separately.
- Possession appears as Dec 2030 target and Dec 2031/RERA possession. Website-safe wording: “2031 onward / RERA possession December 2031”.
- Pricing varies. Use “starting from ₹93 lakh onwards” and gate full pricing behind lead form unless you have a current official cost sheet.
- Some pages use 2–4 BHK, while the official public unit mix includes Studio to 4 Bed + Staff. Use phase labels if narrowing unit mix.

## Remote asset limitation

The official brochure and image URLs have been included in assets/remote_asset_inventory.csv. The environment could not download the remote PDF/images into the ZIP due network/DNS/fetch restrictions, so the ZIP contains the asset inventory and direct URLs rather than local image/PDF binaries.
