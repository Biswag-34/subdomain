## Short project overview

Nikoo Homes 8 brings the garden-living chapter of the Nikoo Homes brand to Bellahalli near Thanisandra Main Road. The project is positioned around a rare mix of landscaped community spaces, modern township conveniences, family-focused planning and access to North Bengaluru’s retail, technology, education and airport corridors.

Rather than selling only apartment sizes, the page should sell the idea of “home is where the garden is”: a lifestyle built around quiet trails, community gardens, children’s play zones, aroma and sensory gardens, woodland seating, sports courts, a clubhouse and swimming pool, and a neighbourhood-first way of life.
